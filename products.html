Comprehensive Banking Solutions

Payments

In today's digital economy, businesses require seamless and secure payment solutions. Our payment offerings include:

  • Payment Gateway: A service that authorizes credit card payments for e-businesses, online retailers, and traditional brick and mortar stores.
  • UPI Payment Gateway: A payment solution that allows users to make real-time payments using their mobile devices via the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).
  • NACH Payments: National Automated Clearing House (NACH) enables bulk payment processing and direct debit transactions, making it easier for businesses to manage their finances.

Banking

We offer a range of advanced banking solutions that cater to various needs:

  • Virtual Account: A digital account solution that allows for easy management of funds without the need for a traditional bank account.
  • ESCROW: A financial arrangement where a third party holds assets on behalf of the transacting parties, offering protection and assurance.
  • Connected Banking: A solution designed to integrate banking services with advanced technologies, enabling more accessible and efficient financial management.

Software Solutions

Our software solutions support a wide array of banking services:

  • Core Banking: Centralized management of accounts and transactions for financial institutions.
  • NBFC: Non-Banking Financial Companies providing a range of banking and financial services without holding a banking license.
  • Loan Management: A system for managing loans, including disbursement, repayment, and tracking.
  • Micro Finance: Financial services tailored for the underserved, offering small loans to help individuals start businesses.
  • Credit Cooperative Society: A cooperative organized for the purpose of providing credit facilities to its members.
  • Treasury Management: Strategies and systems that manage a firm’s liquidity, investments, and risk related to financial activities.
  • Non Profit Accounting: Solutions for managing financials in nonprofit organizations, ensuring compliance and transparency.
  • Corporate Banking: Banking services tailored to corporations, offering checking accounts, loans, and treasury services.
  • Corporate Lending: Loans provided to corporations for various purposes including expansion and operational financing.
  • Corporate Treasury Management: Tools to manage an organization's liquidity, investments, and risk.
  • Business Financing: Solutions aimed at providing funds to businesses for growth, operations, and acquisitions.
